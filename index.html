Instructions

Hi-Lo, or High-Low, is a fairly simple card game. It uses a standard deck of 52 cards, and it has players guessing whether a certain card is higher or lower than the one showing on the table. After each turned card, you can finish the game and take the money or continue and try to win the highest price! But, if you lose, you lose everything ok? Let's make money.
